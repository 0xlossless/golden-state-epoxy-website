From Concrete to Stunning — Here's How It Works

Our proven four-step process ensures every metallic epoxy floor we install is flawless, durable, and breathtaking.

01

Free Consultation

We visit your space, assess the floor, and map out your vision. No pressure, no jargon — just honest conversation about what's possible. As a trusted epoxy floor contractor near you, we make the process simple from day one.

02

Surface Preparation

We grind and diamond-polish your concrete to perfection. Great floors start with great prep — this is the foundation that separates amateur work from a professional metallic epoxy flooring installation.

03

Epoxy Application

Our team pours, spreads, and manipulates the metallic pigments by hand — this is where the magic happens. Each residential or commercial epoxy floor becomes a unique masterpiece.

04

Seal & Cure

A crystal-clear topcoat locks in the design and protects your floor for years to come. The result? A mirror-like finish that makes people ask about your metallic epoxy flooring cost — because it looks like a million bucks.
