Our 4-Layer Garage Floor System — Explained in Plain English

Most failed garage floors don’t fail because epoxy is bad. They fail because the prep is rushed and the system is too thin. Our 4-layer commercial-grade system is built to bond hard, look better, and last longer.

1

We mechanically prep the concrete

We don’t just “clean and coat.” We properly prep the slab so the system can bite into the concrete instead of sitting on top of dust, weak spots, or contamination.

Why it matters: strong prep is what prevents peeling later.
2

We apply a commercial-grade base layer

This creates the foundation of the floor — a thick, durable base that starts building strength and smooth visual coverage across the garage.

Why it matters: this is where cheap one-coat jobs cut corners.
3

We broadcast the decorative finish

Whether you want a premium flake look or a cleaner, high-end finish, this layer gives the floor its style, texture, and custom visual character.

Why it matters: your garage should look intentional, not like an afterthought.
4

We lock it in with a protective topcoat

The final layer helps protect the floor from wear, makes cleanup easier, and gives the finished system the polished, premium look homeowners actually want.

Why it matters: this is what helps your floor stay attractive under real daily use.
